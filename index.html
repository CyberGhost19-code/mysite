Car services are regular maintenance tasks that keep a vehicle running smoothly, safely, and efficiently. These services are important to prevent breakdowns and extend the life of the car. Common car services include: Oil ChangeReplacing old engine oil to keep the engine clean and lubricated.Tire ServicesChecking and adjusting tire pressure, rotating tires, and replacing worn-out one Brake Inspection Making sure the brakes are working properly for safe driving.Air Filter Replacement Changing dirty filters to improve engine performance and air quality AC and Heating Check Ensuring your cars air conditioning and heating systems work properly Regular servicing helps your car perform better, saves fuel, and reduces the risk of costly repairs later.
